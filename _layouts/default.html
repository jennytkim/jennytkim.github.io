Next Top Ad Exec

For the Next Top Ad Exec, I created a experiential marketing campaign for the then-newly launched GM's Maven app. Maven is a car sharing and rental app that's available in select U.S. and Canadian cities. Wayke

During Queen's Hackathon, my team and I created a mobile app that is integrated with the MUSE headband, pebble watch, and Grove so that it monitors your brainwaves.
